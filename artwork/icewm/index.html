IceWM Theme

IceWM is the most comfortable window manager I ever met. It has everything I need and very few I not need.

I especially enjoy the way it can be configured : with $EDITOR. ( Yes, I know there are GUI configurators, but sorry, I do not like them. )

Even more, if your patience allows it, you can draw the theme decorations too with $EDITOR. However, I preferred to edit the images with GIMP first, then only to fine tune them with with text editor when needed.
